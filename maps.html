Phasmophobia

Maps

Maps are the locations you will investigate while completing Contracts. Each map features at minimum 1 Exit Door, 1 Video Camera, and 3 Sinks. Each Location is accessible after collecting and using the Main Door Key. There are 3 different sizes of maps.

Small

  • Bleasdale Farmhouse
  • Edgefield Street House
  • Grafton Farmhouse
  • Ridgeview Road House
  • Tanglewood Street House
  • Willow Street House

Medium

  • Brownstone High School
  • Prison

Large

  • Asylum
